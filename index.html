About

More About Me.

I am a passionate creative in Raleigh, NC working in product design, UI and UX design, Industrial Design, and Photography. I thrive at the intersection of technical knowledge, and working with people. I am a highly conceptual thinker, and I can carry those concepts into detailed implementation.

For the last six years, I have worked intensely to improve my creative process and craft in the realms of product design, software design, and photography.

I am currently at Practichem working as the sole designer in the company. During my time with the company, I worked on numerous projects. Primarily, I worked closely with the software development team to overhaul and expand the primary instrument control software, create a lab organization web app and a ecommerce platform, redesign the marketing website, and create numerous additional tools essential for business functions. I collaborated with the marketing team to generate marketing concepts and then create final deliverables. I designed a new Practichem logo, and slowly updated the overall aesthetic of the brand. Finally, I designed a new frame for a physical instrument, and consulted on solving some of the engineering problems in order to maintain the designed aesthetic. I’ve fully immersed myself in start up culture, and accomplished more than I thought possible.

Services

What I Do.

  • Design Thinking

    I firmly believe that people can solve any large problem by breaking it down into the smallest core problems and then using design thinking to generate solutions. I live for the moment when my team puts the pieces together to generate a new solution to a problem.

  • Product Design

    Products are complicated creatures. I live to tame them. Successful businesses rarely survive with just one thing. The product is more than the thing that the customer buys or a users uses. Products have to be instantly understandable, relateable, and familiar, while also being unique and memorable. How does it do that? Product Design.

  • UX Design

    Products are designed to be used by people, and the best products are created with people as the focus. I just spent two years designing streamlined UX systems for complicated, data-intensive software in an industry that has never before focused on optimal usability. I enjoy tackling the larger problems like these because they force me to go beyond my existing capabilities.

  • Photography

    Photography will always be a passion of mine. When I’m stuck on a design problem, sometimes shooting some photos is the perfect thing to clear my head and add a new flame to my creative process. I primarily shoot portraits, lifestyle imagery, and products, however, I love to branch out and shoot things outside of those topics.
